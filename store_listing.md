## Long Description

✨ **ProWrite AI** — your AI writing assistant, always ready in every text field.

Stop switching tabs to improve your writing. ProWrite AI appears right where you type — emails, LinkedIn posts, Twitter, Google Docs, Notion, Reddit, and anywhere else on the web.

---

### 🚀 Three ways to improve your text

**1. Floating button**
Click any text field and the ProWrite button appears instantly. Choose your tone and language, then hit "Improve" — your text is rewritten right in place.

**2. Right-click menu**
Select any text on any page, right-click, and choose "Improve with ProWrite AI". A polished result panel slides in with Copy and Insert buttons.

**3. Keyboard shortcut**
Press **Ctrl+Shift+P** (Mac: ⌘+Shift+P) to improve the active text field without touching your mouse.

---

### 🎨 Tones & Languages

Choose how you want to sound:
- **Formal** — professional and polished
- **Direct** — clear and concise
- **Persuasive** — compelling and convincing
- **Friendly** — warm and approachable

Output in **12 languages**: Spanish · English · French · German · Portuguese · Italian · Dutch · Polish · Russian · Japanese · Chinese · Arabic

The extension interface automatically adapts to your selected language.

---

### 🗂 Side Panel

Open ProWrite AI as a **persistent side panel** — keep it open on the right while you type on the left. No more losing your place when the popup closes.

---

### 📋 History

Every improvement is saved automatically. Access your last 20 improvements from the History tab — click any entry to reuse it instantly.
